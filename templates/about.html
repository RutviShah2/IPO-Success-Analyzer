About IPO Success Predictor

A smart tool that helps you decide which IPOs are worth applying to — before listing day.

What is This Website?

The IPO Success Predictor is a web application that uses historical IPO data and artificial intelligence to predict whether an upcoming IPO will list at a gain or a loss on the stock exchange.

Every year, thousands of investors apply to IPOs hoping for a listing gain — but many end up losing money because they rely on hype rather than data. This website changes that by giving you a clear, data-driven prediction before you invest.
